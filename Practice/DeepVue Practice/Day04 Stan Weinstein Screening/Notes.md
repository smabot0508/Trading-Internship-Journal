# Stan Weinstein Screening Practice

## Objective

To use DeepVue's screening tools to identify strong stocks based on Stan Weinstein's Stage Analysis methodology.

---

## Observations

I used DeepVue's stock screener to search for stocks displaying characteristics of Stage 2 uptrends. The screening process focused on trend strength, moving average alignment, relative strength, and volume behavior.

As part of the practice, I analyzed AMD and observed that the stock was trading above its major moving averages while maintaining strong momentum and positive price action.

---

## What I Practiced

* Applying Stage Analysis filters.
* Evaluating moving average alignment.
* Observing relative strength.
* Analyzing volume behavior.
* Identifying potential Stage 2 stocks.

---

## Key Learnings

I learned that successful stock screening requires more than simply finding stocks that are moving higher. Strong candidates often exhibit proper trend structure, increasing volume, strong relative strength, and institutional participation.

## Conclusion

The Stan Weinstein Screening practice improved my understanding of stock selection and trend evaluation. DeepVue's screening tools made it easier to identify potential market leaders and apply Stage Analysis concepts in a practical environment.
