# DeepVue Practice – Heatmap

During today's practical session, I explored the Heatmap feature available in DeepVue and learned how traders use it to analyze overall market strength and sector performance. The Heatmap displayed stocks grouped by sectors and industries, allowing me to quickly identify which areas of the market were showing strength and which were experiencing weakness.

I observed sectors such as Information Technology, Health Care, Financials, Industrials, Consumer Discretionary, and Energy. The color-coded visualization made it easy to distinguish strong-performing stocks from weaker-performing stocks. Green-colored stocks indicated positive performance, while red-colored stocks indicated negative performance.

I also noticed how certain industries contained clusters of strong stocks, helping traders identify sector leadership and possible institutional money flow. Rather than analyzing stocks individually, the Heatmap provided a broad market overview within a single screen.

This practice demonstrated how professional traders use Heatmaps to identify strong sectors, monitor market sentiment, discover emerging opportunities, and improve stock selection efficiency.
