# 21 EMA Strategy Practice

## Objective

To apply the 21 EMA Strategy using DeepVue and understand how it can be used to identify trend direction and market strength.

---

## Observations

Using DeepVue, I observed how price interacts with the 21 EMA during different market conditions. Stocks trading above a rising 21 EMA generally displayed strong momentum, while stocks trading below a declining 21 EMA showed weakness.

The strategy helped visualize the difference between aggressive and defensive market environments.

---

## What I Practiced

* Identifying trend direction using the 21 EMA.
* Recognizing aggressive market conditions.
* Recognizing defensive market conditions.
* Observing pullbacks near the 21 EMA.
* Evaluating trend continuation opportunities.

---

## Key Learnings

I learned that the 21 EMA can serve as a dynamic support and resistance level while also helping traders determine whether market conditions are favorable for taking additional risk.

---

## Conclusion

The practical implementation of the 21 EMA Strategy improved my understanding of trend-following techniques and risk management. The strategy provides a simple framework for identifying market strength and potential trading opportunities.
