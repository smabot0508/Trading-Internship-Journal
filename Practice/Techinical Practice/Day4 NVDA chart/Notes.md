# NVDA Chart Analysis

## Objective

To apply Trend Direction, Volume Analysis, and RSI concepts using NVIDIA (NVDA) stock.

---

## Observations

NVDA was analyzed using price action, volume behavior, support and resistance zones, and RSI readings.

The stock remained in an overall uptrend but showed signs of a short-term pullback after approaching resistance.

---

## Trend Analysis

The chart displayed a series of higher highs and higher lows, indicating a bullish trend. Although the stock experienced a recent decline from resistance, the broader trend remained positive.

---

## Support and Resistance

A major support zone was identified near previous buying areas, while resistance was observed near recent highs where selling pressure increased.

---

## Volume Analysis

Volume increased during the bullish rally, confirming strong market participation. Higher volume near resistance indicated increased trading activity and possible profit-taking.

---

## RSI Analysis

RSI remained in the neutral zone, indicating balanced momentum. The stock was neither overbought nor oversold, suggesting that traders were waiting for the next directional move.

---

## Key Learnings

* Trend direction remains the foundation of chart analysis.
* Volume confirms the reliability of price movement.
* RSI helps measure market momentum.
* Multiple indicators provide stronger analysis than relying on a single indicator.

---

## Conclusion

The NVDA analysis provided practical experience in combining trend analysis, volume analysis, and RSI to evaluate market conditions and identify potential trading opportunities.
