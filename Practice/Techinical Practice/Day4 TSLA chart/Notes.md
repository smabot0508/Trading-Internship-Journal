# TSLA Chart Analysis

## Objective

To analyze Tesla (TSLA) using Trend Direction, Volume Analysis, Support and Resistance, and RSI.

---

## Observations

TSLA displayed strong bullish momentum followed by a consolidation phase near resistance. The stock remained in an overall uptrend despite short-term sideways movement.

---

## Trend Analysis

Price action showed a clear pattern of higher highs and higher lows, confirming bullish market structure.

---

## Support and Resistance

Support was identified near previous pullback zones, while resistance was observed near recent highs where price struggled to continue upward.

---

## Volume Analysis

Volume increased during the rally and declined during consolidation. This suggested reduced market participation while traders waited for a breakout or breakdown.

---

## RSI Analysis

RSI remained above 50, indicating positive momentum. However, the indicator did not reach overbought conditions, suggesting that momentum remained healthy.

---

## Key Learnings

* Consolidation often occurs after strong trends.
* Volume helps validate trend continuation.
* RSI can confirm momentum strength.
* Support and resistance remain important decision-making zones.

---

## Conclusion

The TSLA chart provided a strong example of trend continuation followed by consolidation. This analysis improved understanding of trend behavior, momentum analysis, and volume interpretation.
