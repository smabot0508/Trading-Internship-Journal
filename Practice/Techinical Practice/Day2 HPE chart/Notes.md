# HPE Chart Analysis

## Objective

To apply technical analysis concepts such as trend identification, support and resistance, breakout analysis, and volume analysis using HPE stock.

---

## Observations

During chart analysis, HPE displayed strong bullish momentum with consistent upward price movement. The stock showed signs of buyer dominance and increasing market participation.

The following concepts were analyzed:

* Trend Direction
* Support and Resistance
* Breakout Behavior
* Volume Analysis

---

## Trend Analysis

The stock was forming higher highs and higher lows, indicating a bullish trend. Price continued to move upward with strong momentum, suggesting positive market sentiment.

---

## Support and Resistance

Support levels were identified near previous pullback zones where buyers entered the market.

Resistance levels were identified near recent swing highs where price experienced temporary selling pressure.

---

## Volume Analysis

Volume increased during bullish price movement, confirming strong buying participation. The increase in volume added confidence to the continuation of the trend.

---

## Key Learnings

* Trend structure helps identify market direction.
* Volume confirms price movement strength.
* Support and resistance help identify important decision zones.
* Strong trends are often accompanied by increased volume.

---

## Conclusion

The HPE chart provided a practical example of a bullish trend supported by strong volume and positive momentum. This analysis improved understanding of trend identification and volume confirmation.
