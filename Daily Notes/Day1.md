Questions I Had 
1. Why do stocks become highly volatile during market opening? 
2. Why does volume suddenly increase at specific timings? 
3. How do institutional investors affect stock prices? 
 
Answers

orders at the same time. 
 Volume increases when major buying or selling activity happens. 
 Institutional investors move large amounts of money, which strongly impacts 
stock prices and momentum.
