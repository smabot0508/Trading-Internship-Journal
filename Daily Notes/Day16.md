# Questions I Had and Answers I Got

## Q1. Why is revision important in trading?

Revision helps traders strengthen their understanding of concepts and improve their ability to apply them in real market situations. Trading success depends more on consistent execution than constantly learning new concepts.

---

## Q2. Which concept forms the foundation of Smart Money Concepts?

Market Structure is the foundation of Smart Money Concepts because all advanced concepts such as BOS, ChoCh, Liquidity, and Supply & Demand depend on understanding market structure first.

---

## Q3. Why should traders combine multiple concepts instead of using only one?

Using multiple concepts provides confirmation and increases probability. Combining Market Structure, Liquidity, Supply & Demand, and Premium & Discount helps traders make more informed decisions than relying on a single signal.
