# Supply and Demand

## Introduction

Supply and Demand zones are areas on a chart where significant buying or selling activity previously occurred. These zones help traders identify potential areas where price may react in the future.

Supply and Demand are among the most widely used concepts in technical analysis and Smart Money Concepts.

---

## Demand Zones

A Demand Zone is an area where buyers entered aggressively and pushed price upward.

Characteristics:

* Strong bullish reaction.
* Increased buying pressure.
* Potential support area.
* Opportunity for long trades.

Demand zones are typically found at the origin of strong upward moves.

---

## Supply Zones

A Supply Zone is an area where sellers entered aggressively and pushed price downward.

Characteristics:

* Strong bearish reaction.
* Increased selling pressure.
* Potential resistance area.
* Opportunity for short trades.

Supply zones are usually found at the origin of strong downward moves.

---

## How to Draw Zones

### Range Method

Draw around the consolidation area before the breakout move.

### Pivot Method

Draw around the specific candle responsible for the strong directional move.

---

## High-Quality vs Low-Quality Zones

### High-Quality Zones

* Demand in Discount.
* Supply in Premium.

### Low-Quality Zones

* Demand in Premium.
* Supply in Discount.

High-quality zones provide better probabilities because they align with favorable pricing.

---

## Extreme Zones

Extreme zones are located at the beginning of a major swing.

They are important because:

* Institutional orders often originate there.
* They support current market structure.
* Their failure can trigger trend reversals.

---

## Key Takeaways

* Demand = Buying pressure.
* Supply = Selling pressure.
* Trade with trend direction.
* Use Premium and Discount as a filter.
* Extreme zones offer the highest probability.

---

## Conclusion

Supply and Demand zones help traders identify areas where institutional activity may influence price. When combined with Premium and Discount analysis, they become highly effective trading tools.
