# Break of Structure (BOS)

## Introduction

Break of Structure (BOS) is one of the most important concepts in Smart Money Concepts (SMC). It represents a change in the current market structure and often signals a potential shift in trend direction.

A BOS occurs when price stops following the rules of its existing trend and breaks a significant structural level. Traders use BOS to identify trend continuation, trend reversals, and potential trading opportunities.

---

## Understanding Market Structure

Before identifying a BOS, traders must understand how trends are formed.

### Uptrend

An uptrend consists of:

* Higher Highs (HH)
* Higher Lows (HL)

Price continuously creates new highs while maintaining higher lows.

### Downtrend

A downtrend consists of:

* Lower Highs (LH)
* Lower Lows (LL)

Price continuously creates lower highs and lower lows.

---

## How BOS Forms

### BOS in an Uptrend

In an uptrend, the most important level is the most recent Higher Low.

A bearish BOS occurs when:

* Price closes below the most recent Higher Low.
* The sequence of Higher Highs and Higher Lows is broken.

This suggests buyers are losing control and sellers may be taking over.

### BOS in a Downtrend

In a downtrend, the most important level is the most recent Lower High.

A bullish BOS occurs when:

* Price closes above the most recent Lower High.
* The sequence of Lower Highs and Lower Lows is broken.

This suggests sellers are losing control and buyers may be taking over.

---

## The Importance of Candle Body Closures

One of the most critical rules when identifying BOS is waiting for a candle body close.

### Not a BOS

* Price briefly breaks a level with a wick.
* Candle closes back inside the structure.

### Valid BOS

* Candle body closes beyond the structural level.

Wicks alone do not confirm a structural break.

---

## Why BOS Matters

BOS helps traders:

* Identify trend reversals.
* Confirm trend continuation.
* Understand market direction.
* Build trading plans with structure-based logic.

---

## Key Takeaways

* BOS represents a structural shift in the market.
* Uptrends break below Higher Lows.
* Downtrends break above Lower Highs.
* Candle body closures are required for confirmation.
* BOS should be combined with other confluences.

---

## Conclusion

Break of Structure is one of the foundations of Smart Money Concepts. Understanding BOS helps traders objectively identify when market control shifts between buyers and sellers and provides a framework for reading price action with greater confidence.
