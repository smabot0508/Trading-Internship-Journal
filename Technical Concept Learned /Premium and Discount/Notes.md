# Premium and Discount

## Introduction

Premium and Discount is a pricing concept used in Smart Money Concepts (SMC) to determine whether an asset is trading at a relatively expensive or cheap price within a specific swing range.

The concept helps traders avoid buying too high or selling too low by dividing a price range into two distinct areas using the 50% Equilibrium Level.

---

## Understanding Premium and Discount

Every swing range consists of:

* Swing High
* Swing Low

The midpoint between these two levels is known as the Equilibrium Level.

### Premium Zone

The Premium Zone is located above the 50% Equilibrium Level.

Characteristics:

* Price is relatively expensive.
* More selling opportunities exist.
* Supply becomes stronger.
* Traders look for short positions.

### Discount Zone

The Discount Zone is located below the 50% Equilibrium Level.

Characteristics:

* Price is relatively cheap.
* More buying opportunities exist.
* Demand becomes stronger.
* Traders look for long positions.

---

## Why Premium and Discount Matters

Many traders enter trades too early.

For example:

* Buying in Premium often means paying expensive prices.
* Selling in Discount often means selling at cheap prices.

Using Premium and Discount helps traders wait for more favorable pricing.

---

## Applying Premium and Discount

### Step 1: Identify Market Structure

Determine whether the market is bullish or bearish.

### Step 2: Identify Swing Range

Locate the swing high and swing low that define the trend.

### Step 3: Draw Equilibrium

Use a Fibonacci Retracement tool to identify the 50% level.

### Step 4: Wait for Proper Pricing

* Buy in Discount.
* Sell in Premium.

---

## Key Takeaways

* Premium = Expensive.
* Discount = Cheap.
* Equilibrium = 50% midpoint.
* Buy below equilibrium.
* Sell above equilibrium.

---

## Conclusion

Premium and Discount provide traders with a simple but powerful framework for identifying favorable pricing conditions. Combined with market structure and supply and demand, it becomes an effective tool for improving trade quality and reducing unnecessary risk.
