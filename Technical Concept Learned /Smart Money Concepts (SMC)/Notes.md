# Smart Money Concepts (SMC) Introduction

## Introduction

Smart Money Concepts (SMC) is a trading methodology that focuses on understanding how institutional traders, hedge funds, banks, and large market participants influence price movement.

Rather than relying solely on indicators, SMC emphasizes market structure, liquidity, and price action to understand how markets operate.

---

## Purpose of SMC

The objective of SMC is to understand:

* Market Structure
* Liquidity
* Institutional Activity
* Price Manipulation
* Trend Continuation and Reversals

SMC attempts to analyze the market from the perspective of large institutions rather than retail traders.

---

## SMC Learning Roadmap

The general learning path for SMC is:

```text
Market Structure
↓
Break of Structure (BOS)
↓
Change of Character (CHoCH)
↓
Liquidity
↓
Liquidity Sweeps
↓
Imbalance / Fair Value Gap (FVG)
↓
Order Blocks
↓
Mitigation Blocks
↓
Premium & Discount
↓
SMC Trading Models
```

Each concept builds upon the previous one, making it important to understand the fundamentals before progressing to advanced topics.

---

## Why SMC is Popular

Many traders use SMC because it focuses on:

* Understanding price behavior.
* Identifying institutional activity.
* Improving market context.
* Recognizing important liquidity zones.

---

## Key Takeaways

* SMC focuses on institutional market behavior.
* Market Structure is the foundation of SMC.
* Liquidity plays a major role in SMC analysis.
* Advanced concepts build upon basic market structure.

---

## Conclusion

Smart Money Concepts provide a structured approach to analyzing financial markets by focusing on market structure, liquidity, and institutional behavior. Understanding these fundamentals helps traders build a stronger foundation for future learning.
