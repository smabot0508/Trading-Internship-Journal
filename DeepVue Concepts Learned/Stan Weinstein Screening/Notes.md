# Stan Weinstein Screening

## Introduction

Stan Weinstein Screening is a stock selection methodology based on the principles introduced by Stan Weinstein in his Stage Analysis framework. The goal is to identify strong stocks that are entering or currently trading in Stage 2, where the probability of sustained upward movement is highest.

DeepVue provides powerful screening tools that allow traders to apply Stan Weinstein's principles efficiently and discover potential market leaders.

---

## Objective

The main objective of Stan Weinstein Screening is to identify stocks that demonstrate:

* Strong trend direction.
* Positive relative strength.
* Increasing volume.
* Institutional participation.
* Stage 2 characteristics.

By focusing on these factors, traders can concentrate on stocks with the greatest potential for future price appreciation.

---

## Key Screening Criteria

### Trend Strength

Strong stocks typically:

* Trade above major moving averages.
* Form higher highs and higher lows.
* Display consistent upward momentum.

Trend strength is one of the most important factors when evaluating a stock.

---

### Relative Strength

Relative Strength measures how well a stock performs compared to the overall market.

Strong relative strength often indicates:

* Market leadership.
* Institutional buying activity.
* Superior price performance.

Stocks with high relative strength frequently outperform weaker stocks during bullish market conditions.

---

### Moving Average Alignment

A strong stock often displays proper moving average alignment.

Typical characteristics include:

* Price above the 50 SMA.
* Price above the 150 SMA.
* Price above the 200 SMA.
* Rising moving averages.

This alignment confirms a healthy long-term trend.

---

### Volume Confirmation

Volume helps confirm the strength of a stock's movement.

Important observations include:

* Increasing volume during advances.
* Strong participation during breakouts.
* Consistent trading activity.

High volume often indicates institutional involvement.
