# DeepVue Heatmap

## Overview

The DeepVue Heatmap is a market visualization tool that displays stock performance across different sectors and industries using a color-coded format.

The Heatmap allows traders to quickly evaluate overall market conditions, identify sector strength, and discover leading stocks without manually scanning large watchlists.

---

## Purpose

The primary purpose of the Heatmap is to:

* Visualize market performance.
* Identify strong and weak sectors.
* Monitor sector rotation.
* Discover market leaders.
* Improve stock screening efficiency.

---

## How the Heatmap Works

Stocks are grouped by:

* Sector
* Industry
* Market Category

Each stock is represented by a colored block.

### Green Color

Indicates positive price performance.

### Red Color

Indicates negative price performance.

### Color Intensity

The strength of the color often reflects the magnitude of the stock's price movement.

---

## Sector Analysis

The Heatmap allows traders to compare sectors such as:

* Information Technology
* Health Care
* Financials
* Industrials
* Energy
* Consumer Discretionary

This helps traders identify which sectors are attracting buying interest and which sectors are under selling pressure.
