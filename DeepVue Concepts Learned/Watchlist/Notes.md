# DeepVue WatchList

## Introduction

The WatchList feature in DeepVue helps traders organize stocks into customized groups for easier monitoring and analysis. It allows traders to create structured lists based on sectors, trading strategies, market themes, and potential opportunities.

Professional traders often use watchlists to improve efficiency and maintain a systematic approach to stock selection.

---

## Purpose of WatchLists

WatchLists help traders:

* Track multiple stocks efficiently.
* Monitor market leaders.
* Organize stocks by category.
* Reduce research time.
* Improve workflow management.

Instead of repeatedly searching for stocks, traders can access them instantly through organized watchlists.

---

## Types of WatchLists

Common watchlist categories include:

### Market Indexes and Gauges

Used to monitor overall market conditions and important benchmark instruments.

### Leaders

Contains stocks demonstrating strong performance and relative strength.

### Wide List

Includes additional stocks that traders may monitor for future opportunities.

### Ready List

Contains stocks approaching important technical setups or trading opportunities.

## Benefits of WatchLists

WatchLists provide:

* Better organization.
* Faster stock analysis.
* Improved productivity.
* Efficient stock monitoring.
* Structured trading workflow.

Maintaining organized watchlists helps traders focus on high-quality opportunities.
