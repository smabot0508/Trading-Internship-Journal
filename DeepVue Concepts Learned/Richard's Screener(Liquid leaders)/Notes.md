# Richard's Screener (Liquid Leaders Screener)

## Introduction

The Richard's Screener, also known as the Liquid Leaders Screener, is a DeepVue screening tool designed to identify strong, liquid stocks before significant price movements occur.

The screener uses CAN SLIM-inspired principles to reduce thousands of stocks into a focused list of high-quality market leaders.

---

## Purpose

The primary goal of the screener is to:

* Find strong market leaders.
* Identify institutional-quality stocks.
* Reduce scanning time.
* Improve stock selection efficiency.

---

## Core Screening Filters

### High Liquidity

Stocks must have:

* Average Daily Dollar Volume greater than $100 Million.

This ensures sufficient liquidity and institutional participation.

---

### Relative Strength

Stocks should demonstrate strong performance relative to the broader market.

Typical requirements include:

* 1-Month Absolute Strength > 93
* OR
* 3-Month Absolute Strength > 87

---

### Price Requirements

The screener typically filters for:

* Common Stocks
* Last Price greater than $20

These settings can be adjusted according to personal preferences.

---

## Trading Applications

After generating a focused watchlist, traders can scan for:

* Base Breakouts
* Pullbacks to Moving Averages
* Trend Continuation Setups
* Market Leaders

---

## Benefits

* Saves time.
* Identifies strong stocks.
* Improves workflow efficiency.
* Focuses on institutional-quality opportunities.

---

## Conclusion

The Richard's Screener helps traders efficiently identify strong, liquid stocks that demonstrate leadership characteristics and potential for future price expansion.
